Your privacy matters. This policy explains what information Raij Tarot collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

Information We Collect

When you use our booking or testimonial forms, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Service requested and session preferences
  • Any personal context or questions you choose to share

How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely to:

  • Confirm and schedule your session
  • Send your deposit invoice

  • Display approved testimonials on the website (only with your submission)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Data Storage

Form submissions are processed through Web3Forms and delivered directly to our email. We do not operate a database or store your information on external servers beyond what is necessary to respond to your inquiry.

Testimonials

Testimonials submitted through the review form are reviewed before being published. By submitting a testimonial, you give Raij Tarot permission to display it on this website. You may request its removal at any time by contacting us.

Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies or analytics software.

Your Rights

You may request to view, update, or delete any personal information you have submitted by contacting us at carmen@raijtarot.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please reach out at carmen@raijtarot.com.
